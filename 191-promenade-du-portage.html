Building Specifications

Total Square Footage

26,057 sq ft across 6 floors. Designed for large-scale federal and corporate operations.

GoC-Configured Infrastructure

Former PWGSC facility with government-grade telecommunications, security, and access control systems already installed and operational.

Build-Out Status

Zero build-out required. Fully compliant with Government of Canada standards and ready for immediate occupancy.

Lease Rate

$32/sq ft all-in: Base rent $17/sq ft + operating costs $15/sq ft. 50/50 co-brokerage.

Lease Terms

3–10 years, fully negotiable. Flexible terms designed to meet tenant requirements and growth trajectories.

Location

191 Promenade du Portage, Gatineau, Quebec. Gateway to the National Capital Region with direct access to federal offices and institutions.

Who This Building Serves

Federal Tenants

Departments, agencies, and crown corporations seeking dedicated office space with government-grade security and telecommunications already in place. No compliance rework required.

Government Relations Firms

Consultancies and advocacy organizations requiring proximity to federal decision-makers and government stakeholders. 191 Promenade positions you at the center of policy dialogue.

Federal Contractors

Companies bidding on and executing government contracts. The facility's security systems, compliance infrastructure, and location support efficient project delivery and stakeholder engagement.

Policy Organizations

Think tanks, research institutes, and advisory firms working on national policy. Access to government infrastructure and proximity to federal offices enable real-time stakeholder engagement.

International Organizations

Embassies, consulates, and international bodies requiring neutral, secure office space in Canada's National Capital Region.

Corporate Anchors

Large enterprises establishing Canadian operations or expanding federal sales capacity. Government-configured infrastructure reduces onboarding time and deployment risk.
