The Rise of Virtual Offices: 340% Growth in Canada 2019-2023

Virtual office adoption surged 340% from 2019 to 2023 across Canada. The pandemic accelerated this trend by making remote work normal and professional.

Government-adjacent markets like the NCR are seeing even higher adoption. Professionals in policy, government relations, and federal contracting are choosing virtual offices because they allow flexibility while maintaining credibility.

Why Virtual Offices Are Thriving

Hybrid work created demand for flexible address solutions. You need credibility, but you don't need a dedicated desk five days a week. Virtual offices fill exactly this niche.

For government contractors and advisors, the virtual office model is optimal. You get the prestigious address, the meeting room access, the professional infrastructure—and you pay only for what you use.
