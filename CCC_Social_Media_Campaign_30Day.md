## SECTION 1: CAMPAIGN OVERVIEW

### Campaign Name
**"The Government Corridor"** — A 30-day organic social media launch establishing Capital Corridor Campus as the premium professional ecosystem in the National Capital Region.

### Campaign Objective
Drive awareness, inquiries, and trial sign-ups for:
- **Flex space** (Day Pass, Weekly, Monthly)
- **Virtual office services** (Address, Phone, Executive)
- **Property leasing** (full floors, executive suites)

Target 200+ follower growth, 40+ website visits, 8–12 qualified inquiries.

### Campaign Mechanism
Organic content only — no paid media. Focus: educate NCR business community on why proximity to Parliament matters, show proof (video, photos, testimonials), drive action (book a tour, reserve day pass, contact for leasing).

### Platforms & Posting Cadence
| Platform | Posts/Week | Purpose |

## SECTION 2: CONTENT PILLARS

### Content Pillar 1: Location Intelligence
**Objective:** Establish the "why" — proximity to Parliament, federal corridor, policy ecosystem.  
**Frequency:** 2x per week (Instagram + LinkedIn)  
**Post types:**
- Drone shots / street-level photos showing walking distance to Parliament Hill
- Map graphics: "6-minute drive to Parliament / Supreme Court / Place du Portage"
- Data posts: "Why the National Capital Region's elite choose Promenade du Portage"
- Testimonial snippets from government affairs officers

**Why it matters:** Differentiator from Regus/WeWork. Government relations firms, lobbyists, federal contractors need physical proximity to decision-makers.

---

### Content Pillar 2: Property Showcase
**Objective:** Build emotional connection. Show the buildings are premium, modern, institutional.  
**Frequency:** 2x per week (Instagram carousel + Facebook full gallery)  
**Post types:**
- 4 exterior photos per building (daylight, evening, signage, street-level)
- Floor plan highlights with suite names (Étage Concorde, Suite du Cabinet, etc.)
- Virtual tour clips (15–30 sec) extracted from full YouTube tour
- Before/after renovations (if available) or architectural details

**Why it matters:** Skepticism of "coworking" stigma. Premium aesthetics overcome objection. LEED Gold, heritage building positioning reinforces institutional credibility.

---

### Content Pillar 3: Client Use Cases & Personas
**Objective:** Make the reader (target prospect) the hero. Show how similar professionals use CCC.  
**Frequency:** 2x per week (LinkedIn + Facebook)  
**Post types:**
- Scenario-based posts: "You're a lobbyist. Parliament votes Wednesday. Where do you meet your team?"
- Federal contractor angle: "Gatineau tax advantage + Parliament proximity = competitive edge"
- Law firm satellite office: "When your Toronto HQ needs a National Capital presence"
- Consultant spotlight: "Why solo practitioners choose Promenade du Portage"
- Government relations firm mini-case: "How proximity to Place du Portage reduced response time from 48h to 4h"

**Why it matters:** StoryBrand principle: reader is the hero. Lowers perceived risk. Makes pricing and services feel relevant, not generic.

---

### Content Pillar 4: Behind-the-Scenes & Building Heritage
**Objective:** Create prestige, humanize the brand, build emotional narrative.  
**Frequency:** 1x per week (Instagram Stories + Facebook post)  
**Post types:**
- Heritage story: "179 Promenade — A LEED Gold restoration of Canada's government corridor tradition"
- Staff perspective: "Meet the team keeping Capital Corridor Campus operational 24/7"
- Customer journey video: Time-lapse of a client's first day (desk setup, meeting room, coffee)
- Historical context: "Promenade du Portage has been the policy epicenter for 30+ years. Here's why."
- Building features deep-dive: "Why we chose LEED Gold certification for every tenant"

**Why it matters:** Builds institutional identity. Differentiates from transactional coworking. Positions Joseph and CCC as stewards, not landlords.

---

### Content Pillar 5: Market Education & Tax/Advantages
**Objective:** Position Gatineau advantage. Educate on why this location matters strategically.  
**Frequency:** 1x per week (LinkedIn + Facebook)  
**Post types:**
- "Why federal contractors choose Gatineau: proximity + Quebec R&D tax credits"
- "The capital gains advantage: Incorporating in Ontario + operating from Gatineau"
- "Why your government relations team needs a National Capital presence (not a virtual office)"
- "The Promenade du Portage economy: Where policy meets private enterprise"
- "4 reasons lobbying firms are consolidating on Promenade du Portage"

**Why it matters:** Educates without selling. Builds authority. Attracts inbound organic search traffic. Makes CCC the obvious choice.
