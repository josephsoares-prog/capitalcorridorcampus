Privacy Policy

How we protect your information

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

  • Contact Forms: Name, email, phone, inquiry details
  • Email Inquiries: Sender email, message content
  • Leasing Inquiries: Contact information, property interests
  • Analytics: Pages visited, time on site, device type (Google Analytics 4)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested information
  • Facilitate leasing processes and property viewings
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. GA4 collects data such as:

  • Pages viewed and navigation patterns
  • Session duration and engagement metrics
  • Device type and browser information

For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. CASL Compliance (Canada)

We comply with Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation (CASL). If you provide your email address, you consent to receive:

  • Responses to your specific inquiry
  • Updates about requested properties
  • Related leasing information

You may unsubscribe at any time by replying to any email with 'unsubscribe' or contacting us directly.

5. CAN-SPAM Compliance (USA)

If you are a USA-based user, we comply with the CAN-SPAM Act. All commercial emails include:

  • Accurate subject line
  • Clear identification as a message from Capital Corridor Campus
  • Physical address and unsubscribe instructions

6. GDPR and International Privacy

If you are located in the European Union, we acknowledge GDPR requirements. We:

  • Collect personal data only with valid consent
  • Provide transparency about how your data is used
  • Honor your right to access, correct, or delete your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@campuscorridor.ca.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure. You submit information at your own risk.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Complete the transaction or purpose for which it was collected
  • Comply with legal or regulatory requirements
  • Resolve disputes or enforce agreements

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data:

  • Email: info@campuscorridor.ca
  • Phone: 873-455-3667
  • Address: 179 Promenade du Portage, Gatineau, QC

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective when posted on this page. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
