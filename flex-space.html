Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a Day Pass? ▼

A Day Pass includes access to a private office or hot desk, high-speed WiFi, 1 hour of meeting room access, printing services, and complimentary coffee. It's ideal for consultants making short visits to the National Capital Region or teams handling overflow work.

Can I upgrade from a Day Pass to a longer commitment? ▼

Absolutely. Day pass credits can be applied toward any weekly or monthly package. If you've used 3 day passes and decide to commit to a week, we'll credit the $225 toward your first weekly payment. Contact info@campuscorridor.ca to arrange an upgrade.

Is 24/7 access available on the weekly plan? ▼

The Weekly package includes after-hours access until 10 PM and early-morning access starting at 6 AM. Full 24/7 access (including weekends and holidays) is available exclusively with the Monthly Membership plan.

Can my business use the address as a registered office? ▼

Yes. Business address use (for mail and corporate registration) is included in both the Weekly and Monthly plans. Mail handling is limited in the Weekly plan (weekly pickup) and unlimited in the Monthly plan (daily management). Consult with your accountant regarding registration requirements in your jurisdiction.

What are the security measures? ▼

All spaces include government-grade security: 24/7 closed-circuit video surveillance, controlled access with key card systems, secure visitor management protocols, and on-site security presence. The building maintains the security standards required for federal government office space. Sensitive documents can be stored in secure lockable cabinets.

Are there restrictions on the types of work? ▼

Flex space is available for legitimate business purposes only. We welcome government contractors, consulting firms, policy organizations, legal advisors, NGOs, and technology companies. Activities that violate Canadian law or would damage our reputation with government partners are not permitted. Contact us to discuss your specific business model.

Can I book meeting rooms in advance? ▼

Yes. Weekly members receive 5 hours per week of meeting room access with advance booking available up to 2 weeks in advance. Monthly members have unlimited meeting room access with priority booking. Day pass holders can book up to 1 hour on the day of use, subject to availability.

What if I need to cancel? ▼

Day passes are non-refundable once redeemed. Weekly packages can be cancelled with 1 week notice. Monthly memberships require 30 days notice for cancellation. No early-termination fees apply. Contact info@campuscorridor.ca to initiate cancellation.
