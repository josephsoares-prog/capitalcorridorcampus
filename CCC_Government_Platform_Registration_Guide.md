## PLATFORM 2: SEAO (Quebec Provincial Procurement)

**URL:** https://www.seao.ca
**Cost:** FREE for basic registration (paid subscription for bid submission — not needed yet)
**Time:** ~10 minutes
**Why:** Quebec provincial ministries needing Gatineau office space go through SQI, which posts on SEAO. Your Make.com SEAO monitor (Scenario 4468311) scans every 6 hours — registration gets you full access to respond.

### Step-by-Step

1. Go to: **https://www.seao.ca**
2. Click **"Inscription"** (Registration) — top right area
3. Select profile type: **"Entreprise privée"** (Private company / Supplier)
4. **Basic Information — Step 1:**
   - NEQ: **1177153302**
   - Legal name: **IBPROM Corp** (should auto-populate from NEQ)
   - Address: 1 Rideau Street, Suite 700, Ottawa ON K1N 8S7
   - Contact: Joseph Soares
   - Email: j@josephsoares.com
   - Phone: 613-691-9311
5. **Create your user credentials:**
   - User code: Choose something neutral (don't use your name — SEAO recommends a neutral code). Example: **ibprom2026**
   - Password: 8–14 characters, must include at least 2 non-letter characters
   - **Your user code is permanent and cannot be changed** — choose carefully
6. **Business sector / Categories:**
   - Search and select: **"Immobilier"**, **"Location de bureaux"**, **"Services immobiliers"**
   - These determine what tender notifications you receive
7. **Subscription selection:**
   - Choose **"Non-subscribed supplier"** (free tier) for now

   - **Do NOT pay for a subscription yet** — you're monitoring, not bidding. Upgrade only when a matching opportunity appears.
8. **Accept Terms of Use**
9. **Complete registration**
10. **Set up notification profile:**

**You're done.** Between SEAO's own alerts and your Make.com monitor, you'll catch any Quebec government office space opportunity in the Outaouais region.
