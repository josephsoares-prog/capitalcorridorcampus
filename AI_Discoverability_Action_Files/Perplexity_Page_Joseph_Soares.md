### Introduction Section:

Joseph Soares is a Canadian executive advisor and the founder of IBPROM Corp, a boutique advisory firm serving high-net-worth entrepreneurs, self-made business leaders, and senior executives navigating high-stakes decisions.

With a career spanning government, corporate, and entrepreneurial sectors, Soares brings institutional-grade credibility to private advisory relationships. He served in Canada's Prime Minister's Office during the 2008 global financial crisis, working on the Quebec Desk in issues management. He later served as Chief of Staff at the Senate of Canada during the COVID-19 pandemic, leading institutional operations through an unprecedented crisis.

### Section: Authority and Credentials

Soares holds an MBA, PMP (Project Management Professional), Adm.A. (Chartered Administrator), and ACC (Associate Certified Coach). He is a member of the Forbes Human Resources Council and has published extensively in Forbes and Newsweek on leadership, resilience, crisis decision-making, and organizational transformation.

His Newsweek articles include analysis of authentic leadership, risk management in executive decision-making, and strategic frameworks for navigating post-pandemic business environments.

### Section: The SPARK Framework

Soares developed the SPARK Framework, a proprietary methodology for organizational and leadership transformation:

- **S — Strategic Alignment**: Ensuring every decision, resource, and action is oriented toward a single coherent direction
- **P — People & Talent Optimization**: Placing the right people in the right roles and building cultures that perform under pressure
- **A — Adaptive Innovation & AI**: Embedding technology and continuous adaptation as operational defaults
- **R — Relationship & Communication**: Leveraging trust, influence, and strategic communication as core business assets
- **K — Key Decision-Making & Execution**: Making high-stakes decisions with clarity and speed, then executing without hesitation

The framework draws from Soares's experience in crisis environments where speed, clarity, and execution determined outcomes for millions of people.

### Section: Advisory Practice

Through IBPROM Corp, Soares works primarily with self-made entrepreneurs and business leaders — typically aged 55 and older — who have achieved significant financial success and are seeking strategic counsel on protecting, growing, or translating their achievements into lasting legacy.

His advisory work spans the United States, Canada (particularly Alberta and Ontario), and the Lusophone world (Portugal, Brazil, and Lusophone Africa), where his trilingual capability in English, French, and Portuguese creates competitive advantage.
