Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and tracking technologies

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit and improve your user experience.

2. Cookies We Use

Capital Corridor Campus uses the following types of cookies:

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) Cookies

We use Google Analytics 4 to analyze website traffic and user behavior. GA4 sets cookies to:

  • Track unique visitors across sessions
  • Record page views and user interactions
  • Measure engagement and conversion events
  • Improve website functionality based on usage patterns

GA4 Measurement ID: G-L3TWNPP48M

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to deliver typeface assets. For details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics and Google Fonts are third-party services. These vendors may use cookies to:

  • Deliver analytics and insights
  • Improve their services and products
  • Detect fraudulent activity

For more information, review Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Allow specific cookies while blocking others
  • Clear cookies periodically
  • Receive notifications when a cookie is set

Note: Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Browser Cookie Settings

Learn how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding cookies:

  • GDPR (EU): Right to consent, access, and withdraw consent
  • CASL (Canada): Right to withdraw consent for marketing cookies
  • CCPA (California): Right to opt-out of data sales and tracking
